Our Resturant and Cuisine

At Ocean Paradise, we offer a wide range of cuisines. Experience the serenity of the beach and the sound of waves at Chadni Lounge, with fresh and rustic flavors of grilled dishes. Indulge in our spectacular varieties of cuisine at the Curry Leaf restaurant. A warm cup of coffee with cookies has to be the best combination, live your lazy evenings at Kolatoli Cafe. Relax your body and munch on a snack with fresh juice at our Pool Cafe.

Accommodations of Ocean Paradise

Wake up to the sunlight peeking into your spacious and elegantly designed rooms at Ocean Paradise. The enchanting sea and mountain view rooms capture your heart and make your stay special. We have Deluxe rooms, Creative Studio rooms, Suites and more rooms composed solely for the purpose of your comfort and tranquility.

The Pool and Spa Experience

Have it your way when it comes to relaxation and rejuvenation of your body and mind. Enjoy your time with mouthwatering snacks from the Pool Cafe or take a cool dip in the pool. Give your body and soul a treat at our Spa Essenza, with your preferred body massages and treatment packages.
